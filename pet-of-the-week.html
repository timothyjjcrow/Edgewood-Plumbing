Because even plumbers love furry (and scaly, and feathery) friends!

This Week's Star: Whiskers

Meet Whiskers!

Owner: The Johnson Family

Favorite Toy: Anything with feathers

Quirky Habit: Sits in the sink while we brush our teeth

Whiskers is a 3-year-old rescue cat who has become the unofficial supervisor of all plumbing work at the Johnson household. Whenever we visit for a repair, Whiskers is right there inspecting our work and making sure everything is done to the highest standard. Despite being quite particular about her water dish, she's the sweetest companion who loves to greet all visitors with a friendly meow.
